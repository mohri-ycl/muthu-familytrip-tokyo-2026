Take the Airport Limousine Bus directly from Narita to Roppongi. No train transfers, no heavy luggage on stairs — just sit back and relax. The bus stops directly at or near Grand Hyatt Tokyo.

Drop off luggage, freshen up. Grand Hyatt is in the heart of Roppongi — one of Tokyo's best dining districts, so dinner options are just steps away.

📞 +81-3-4333-1234
~20:00

Roppongi has excellent dining for all tastes. Recommended options within walking distance of Grand Hyatt:

04

🗻 Mt. Fuji Day Trip

Monday, May 25 · ☀️ 0% rain · BEST weather day — don't reschedule!

☀️ May 25 is forecast at 0% rain with sunny skies at Kawaguchiko — the best window all week. Mt. Fuji is clearest in the early morning, so an early departure is essential.
6:30 AM

🚂 Depart Shinjuku

Take the Limited Express "Fuji Excursion" (富士回遊) direct from Shinjuku to Kawaguchiko. No transfer needed. Book reserved seats in advance.

🚇 Limited Express Fuji Excursion from Shinjuku ~2 hours · ~¥4,130 reserved seat 🎟 Book Train (Eki-net) Or Highway Bus
8:30 AM

🏔️ Kawaguchiko Lakeside

The lakeside provides the iconic Mt. Fuji reflection shot (Sakasa Fuji). Best light is early morning before clouds form around midday. Walk the northern shore for best angles.

📍 3641 Funatsu, Fujikawaguchiko, Yamanashi 401-0301
📍 2-min walk from Kawaguchiko Station
10:30 AM

💧 Oshino Hakkai (Eight Springs)

8 crystal-clear spring-fed ponds fed by Mt. Fuji snowmelt. Beautiful traditional village scenery. Great for photos and a relaxed 45-minute wander. Try the soft serve ice cream!

📍 Oshino, Minamitsuru District, Yamanashi 401-0511
🚌 Bus from Kawaguchiko Station ~15 min
1:00 PM

⛰️ Mt. Fuji 5th Station

Take the Fuji Subaru Line Bus up to 2,300m elevation. Even if you don't climb, the views are spectacular and the alpine air is refreshing. Bring a light jacket — it can be 10°C colder than the lake.

📍 Fuji Subaru Line 5th Station, Fujiyoshida, Yamanashi
🚌 Bus from Kawaguchiko Station → ~50 min · ¥1,500 round trip
5:00 PM

🚂 Return to Shinjuku

Board the return Fuji Excursion train from Kawaguchiko Station. Arrive Shinjuku ~7 PM.

Tuesday, May 26 · 🌤️ 40% rain

10:00 AM

⚡ Akihabara Electric Town

The world capital of anime, manga, gaming, and electronics. Multi-floor stores with collectibles, figures, and rare merchandise. Arcades, claw machines, themed cafés. Give the 16-yr-old 2–3 hours of free roam here — absolute heaven.

📍 Akihabara, Taito City / Chiyoda City, Tokyo
🚇 JR Chuo-Sobu Line from Shinjuku → Akihabara Stn (Electric Town Exit) ~20 min · ¥220
2:00 PM

🐼 Ueno Zoo

One of Japan's oldest and best urban zoos, home to giant pandas. The 12-yr-old will love the panda area, red pandas, and penguins. Combine with a stroll through Ueno Park afterward.

📍 9-83 Uenokoen, Taito City, Tokyo 110-8711
🚇 JR Yamanote Line from Akihabara → Ueno Stn · ~3 min · ¥160 Open Tue–Sun · 9:30 AM–5:00 PM · Closed Mondays
5:30 PM

🎮 Evening: Back to Akihabara

Return to Akihabara for night arcades (open until midnight), dinner at themed restaurants, and any last shopping. Retro Game Camp and Super Potato are must-visits for the teens.

💡 Tips for Your Trip

🍛 Vegetarian Food

Say "bejitarian desu" (I'm vegetarian). Indian restaurants in Shinjuku Nishishinjuku, Ueno, and Nishi-Kasai (Little India). 7-Eleven/FamilyMart have salads and onigiri.

💳 IC Card (Suica)

Buy at Narita or Haneda airport immediately. Load ¥3,000–5,000. Works on all trains, subways, buses, and convenience stores. No need to buy individual tickets.

🌧️ Rainy Days

Days 2, 3, 6, 7 have rain. Pack light compact umbrellas or ponchos. DisneySea, Harry Potter, and teamLab are all fully indoor — rain doesn't affect them.

📱 Data / Wi-Fi

Buy a Japan eSIM before departure (Airalo, IIJmio). Or get a pocket Wi-Fi on arrival. Having data is essential for navigation, Disney app, and tickets.

🎢 Disney Premier Access

Download the TDR official app before arriving. Once inside the park, use it immediately to book Premier Access (paid skip-the-line) for Beauty & the Beast and Journey to Center of Earth.

👗 teamLab Dress Code

Wear pants or shorts — many rooms have mirrored floors and low lighting. They provide modesty covers if needed. Comfortable walking shoes recommended. No stilettos.

🗻 Mt. Fuji Weather

Check isfujivisible.com the morning of May 25. If cloudy at 7 AM, it likely won't clear. Depart Shinjuku by 6:30 AM for best views.

🏪 Drugstore Shopping

Matsumoto Kiyoshi and Don Quijote in Shinjuku for skincare, snacks, and souvenirs. Great for last-day shopping (Day 7). Popular buys: Hada Labo, medicated patches, Kit Kat flavors.

🌡️ May Weather

Average 18–26°C. Can be humid. Light layers for Mt. Fuji (bring jacket for 5th Station — it can be 10°C colder). Comfortable walking shoes essential — you'll walk 15,000+ steps daily.
